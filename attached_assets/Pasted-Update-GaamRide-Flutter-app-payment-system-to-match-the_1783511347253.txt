Update GaamRide Flutter app payment system to match the real business model:
SAATHI COLLECTS PAYMENT FROM CUSTOMER DIRECTLY.
The app does NOT process money. No Razorpay, no gateway, no SDK.

═══ REAL PAYMENT MODEL ═══

CASH FLOW:
  Customer hands cash to saathi after ride → Saathi taps "Cash received" in app
  → Firestore updated → Both see ride complete

ONLINE UPI FLOW (Saathi's personal UPI):
  Customer wants to pay online → Saathi shows their personal UPI QR code in app
  → Customer scans with any UPI app (GPay/PhonePe/Paytm) on their phone
  → Customer pays saathi directly (peer-to-peer, no app commission)
  → Saathi sees payment in their bank → Taps "Payment received" in app
  → Firestore updated → Both see ride complete

THE APP'S ONLY JOB:
  - Let customer choose payment method at booking time (cash or online)
  - Show saathi's UPI QR when customer chooses online
  - Let saathi CONFIRM they received payment (both methods)
  - Save payment status to Firestore for admin visibility

WHAT NOT TO BUILD:
  - NO Razorpay SDK or any payment gateway
  - NO card payments (not relevant for rural Gujarat)
  - NO app-to-saathi transfer
  - NO deduction of commission from fare (commission model is future scope)

── lib/screens/saathi/upi_qr_screen.dart ──

Widget that shows saathi's UPI QR for customer to scan.

HOW UPI QR WORKS:
  A standard UPI deep link encodes the saathi's UPI ID, name, and amount.
  Any UPI app (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm) can scan it and auto-fill the payment.
  Format: upi://pay?pa={upiId}&pn={upiName}&am={amount}&cu=INR&tn=GaamRide

BUILD THE QR:
  String upiId = saathiData['upiId'] ?? '';
  String upiName = saathiData['upiName'] ?? saathiData['name'] ?? 'Saathi';
  String amount = fare.toStringAsFixed(2);

  String upiString =
    'upi://pay?pa=$upiId&pn=${Uri.encodeComponent(upiName)}'
    '&am=$amount&cu=INR&tn=GaamRide+Ride+Payment';

DISPLAY WITH qr_flutter:
  QrImageView(
    data: upiString,
    version: QrVersions.auto,
    size: 240.0,
    backgroundColor: Colors.white,
    padding: EdgeInsets.all(16),
  )

FULL WIDGET UI:
  White card (rounded 20px, shadow sm), centered:
    UPI logo or "Scan to pay" label (12sp gray, above QR)
    QrImageView (240×240)
    Dotted border container around QR (dashed orange border, rounded 12px)
    Below QR: saathi name + UPI ID in small gray text
    Fare badge: "₹{fare}" — large, bold orange, centered below QR
    Small icons row: GPay logo + PhonePe logo + Paytm logo

── EDGE CASES TO HANDLE ──

1. SAATHI COMPLETES RIDE, CUSTOMER DISPUTES:
   Admin can manually set paymentStatus = 'collected' or 'disputed'
   Add 'disputed' as a valid paymentStatus in admin panel

2. RIDE CANCELLED — no payment needed:
   If status = 'cancelled', skip PaymentConfirmScreen entirely
   paymentStatus stays 'pending' (irrelevant for cancelled rides)

3. APP CLOSED MID-PAYMENT:
   On saathi app open, check for any rides where:
   status == 'completed' AND paymentConfirmedBySaathi == false
   If found, show a banner: "You have an unconfirmed payment — tap to confirm"
   This prevents payment being forgotten if app closes

   In saathi home screen initState:
   final unpaidRides = await FirebaseFirestore.instance
     .collection('rides')
     .where('saathiId', isEqualTo: currentUser.uid)
     .where('status', isEqualTo: 'completed')
     .where('paymentConfirmedBySaathi', isEqualTo: false)
     .limit(1)
     .get();

   if (unpaidRides.docs.isNotEmpty) {
     final ride = unpaidRides.docs.first;
     showPendingPaymentBanner(ride.id, ride.data());
   }

4. HAUL BOOKINGS — same logic:
