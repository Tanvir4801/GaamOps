
═══ lib/screens/customer/payment_screen.dart ═══

Build a beautiful payment selection screen for GaamRide.

CONSTRUCTOR PARAMS:
  PaymentScreen({
    required String rideId,
    required double fare,
    required String pickupVillage,
    required String destinationVillage,
    required double distanceKm,
    required String customerName,
    required String customerPhone,
  })

SCREEN STRUCTURE (Scaffold, no AppBar — custom header):

1. CUSTOM HEADER (orange bg #f97316):
   - Back arrow (white, left)
   - Title: "Choose payment" (white, 16sp, weight 500)
   - Subtitle: "{pickupVillage} → {destinationVillage} • {distanceKm}km" (white, 12sp, 70% opacity)

2. FARE SUMMARY CARD (white card, -12px top margin to overlap header, mx:12, rounded 14px, shadow):
   Left side:
     - Label: "Total fare" (11sp, gray)
     - Amount: "₹{fare}" (28sp, bold, black)
   Right side:
     - "{distanceKm} km" (11sp, orange)
     - "Base ₹20 + ₹8/km" (10sp, gray)

3. SECTION LABEL: "Online payment" (10sp, uppercase, gray, px:16, pt:16)

4. PAYMENT OPTIONS LIST (StatefulWidget, _selectedMethod tracks selection):

   Each option row:
     - height: 58px
     - Leading icon (36×36 rounded container, branded color)
     - Title (13sp, medium) + subtitle (10sp, gray)
     - Trailing: custom radio button (18px circle, orange when selected)
     - onTap: setState(() => _selectedMethod = method)
     - Selected bg: Color(0xFFFFF7ED) (light orange tint)

   OPTIONS:
   a) Google Pay
      icon: white box border, "G" blue + "P" green text
      title: "Google Pay"
      subtitle: "Pay via GPay UPI"
      badge: small pill "POPULAR" (orange bg, white text, 9sp)
      method: 'gpay'

   b) PhonePe
      icon: purple (#5f259f) box, "Pe" white text
      title: "PhonePe"
      subtitle: "Pay via PhonePe UPI"
      method: 'phonepe'

   c) Paytm
      icon: dark blue (#002970) box, "Pay" cyan text
      title: "Paytm"
      subtitle: "Paytm wallet or UPI"
      method: 'paytm'

   d) Any UPI app
      icon: light gray box, "UPI" green text
      title: "Any UPI app"
      subtitle: "Enter your UPI ID manually"
      method: 'upi'

5. THIN DIVIDER (mx:16, color gray 200)

6. SECTION LABEL: "Offline"

7. CASH OPTION:
   icon: light green box, money emoji
   title: "Cash"
   subtitle: "Pay saathi directly after ride"
   method: 'cash'
   NOTE: No fake "processing" — cash is honest and immediate

8. BOTTOM AREA (pinned at bottom, px:16, pb:24):
   PAY BUTTON:
     height: 52px, full width, borderRadius: 14px
     bg: #f97316 (orange) for online methods
     bg: #16a34a (green) for cash
     text: "Pay ₹{fare} with {method name}" (online)
           "Confirm cash payment" (cash)
     font: 15sp, white, weight 600
     onPressed: _processPayment()

   TRUST BADGE (below button, center):
     "🔒 Secured by Razorpay" (10sp, gray) — only show for online methods
     For cash: "No app charges. Pay directly to saathi." (10sp, gray)

LOADING STATE:
   When payment is processing: show CircularProgressIndicator over the button
   Disable button tap during processing (bool _isProcessing)
